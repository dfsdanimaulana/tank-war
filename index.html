Tank War Game Guide

Controls

  • Use the arrow keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) or the WASD keys to move your tank.
  • Press Control key or C to boost your player speed.
  • Press the Enter key or Spacebar to shoot your weapon.

    • Bonuses

    In the game, you can collect different bonuses to enhance your tank's capabilities:

  • Red Bonus: This bonus upgrades your weapon.
  • Blue Bonus: Collecting a blue bonus gives you an extra life.
  • Yellow Bonus: This bonus provides a shield to protect your tank.
  • White Bonus: The white bonus grants you a powerful rocket weapon.

Use these bonuses wisely to conquer your enemies and achieve victory in Tank War!
